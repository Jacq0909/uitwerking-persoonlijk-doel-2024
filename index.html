10 eCommerce
best practices

Create a good first impression: decide which style suits best, streamline navigation, make sure it looks good across all devices (don’t forget mobile!), and optimize the website. Optimize by fast loading, no broken links, all info easy to access.

Build trust with your target audience | use trust signals (quality logo’s)

Showcase your products with stunning visuals | use high-quality photos, on a white background, upload three or more of the same products from various angles.

Build a community for your customers | create a space where people can share the same interests or network
